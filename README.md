## New In-App Purchase and subscription data now available in Analytics  

###### March 25, 2026  
<div class="article-text"><div class="inline-article-image"><img alt="" data-hires="false" data-img-dark="https://devimages-cdn.apple.com/wwdc-services/articles/images/EDB4AFA0-7888-44A8-9004-78F906E586AB/2048.jpeg" src="https://devimages-cdn.apple.com/wwdc-services/articles/images/EDB4AFA0-7888-44A8-9004-78F906E586AB/2048.jpeg"/></div><p>Analytics in AppStoreConnect receives its biggest update since its launch, including a refreshed user experience that makes it easier to measure the performance of your apps and games. Updates include:</p><ul>
<li>More than 100 new metrics. Now you can access monetization and subscription data in Analytics to better understand the performance of your In-App Purchases and offers.</li>
<li>New cohort capabilities. Analyze user behavior based on common attributes — such as download date, download source, offer start date, and more — to measure how a particular group of users performs over time. For example, if you’ve expanded your app to a new region, you can monitor how long it takes users in that region to make a purchase compared to other more established regions. Cohort data is aggregated to ensure user privacy.</li>
<li>New peer group benchmarks. Discover how you stack up to peers with two new monetization benchmarks: download-to-paid conversion and proceeds per download. Benchmarks incorporate differential privacy techniques to protect individual developer performance while also providing meaningful and actionable insights.</li>
<li>Two new subscription reports. Export these via the Analytics Reports API to perform offline analysis and integrate Analytics into your own data systems.</li>
<li>Additional filters. Apply up to seven filters to your selected metrics at once allowing you to drill down further and uncover additional insights.</li>
<li>AppStore Analytics Guide. This new guide in AppStore Connect Help enables you to develop a data-driven strategy and understand AppStore tools and features you can use to grow your business.</li>
</ul><p><a href="http://developer.apple.com/app-store-connect/analytics/">Learn about measuring performance <span class="icon icon-after icon-chevronright nowrap">with Analytics</span></a></p><p><a href="https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect-analytics/">Read the new <span class="icon icon-after icon-chevronright nowrap">Analytics guide</span></a> </p></div>
